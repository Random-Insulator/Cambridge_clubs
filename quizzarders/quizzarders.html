Quizzarders.

The Quizzarders are Cambridge Court's competitive quiz team. We train weekly across all subjects — science, history, geography, literature, pop culture, sport, and current affairs. Every term we compete in inter-school quiz leagues and national competitions. Last year we reached the national school quiz finals for the first time in the school's history. We also run the annual school quiz night, which is one of the most popular events in the calendar. If you love knowing things, learning things, and the thrill of a buzzer round, this is your club.
