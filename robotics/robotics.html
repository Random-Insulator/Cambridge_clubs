Technology Every Monday · Tech Lab B

Robotics.

The Robotics Club builds things that move, think, and compete. We design and program robots using Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and LEGO Mindstorms — from line-following bots to autonomous navigation systems. Each year we enter the national school robotics competition and have placed in the top 10 for the last three years. You don't need any experience to join — we'll teach you everything from soldering to Python. If you like making things and breaking things to understand how they work, this is your club.
