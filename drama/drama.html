Theatre.

The Theatre Club is Cambridge Court's creative performance hub. Every year we produce a full-scale school production — past shows include Grease, A Midsummer Night's Dream, and an original student-written play. We also run improv nights, monologue workshops, and enter regional Theatre festivals. No audition is required to join — there are roles both on stage and behind the scenes including directing, set design, costumes, lighting, and sound. Theatre builds confidence, teamwork, and self-expression. Whatever your background, there's a place for you here.
