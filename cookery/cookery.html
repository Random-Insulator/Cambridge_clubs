Cookery.

The Cookery Club focuses on fireless cooking, where students prepare dishes like salads, sweets, sandwiches, and tacos using sustainable, local ingredients. Members master essential culinary skills such as cutting, plating, and presentation while learning about nutrition, hygiene, and waste reduction. Through interactive activities, nutrition quizzes, and theme-based competitions, students collaborate to create innovative meals, fostering both teamwork and creativity in a safe, hands-on environment.
