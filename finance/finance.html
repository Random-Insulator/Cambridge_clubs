Finance Every Wednesday · Room 22

Finance.

The Finance Club teaches students how money actually works — before they have to figure it out alone. We cover personal budgeting, investing, the stock market, entrepreneurship, and how to read financial news. Members run a simulated investment portfolio and compete in national student stock market challenges. We've also hosted guest talks from bankers, startup founders, and a venture capitalist. Whether you want to start a business, understand the economy, or just stop being confused by money, this club will change how you see the world.
